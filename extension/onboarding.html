Welcome to JITTER

JITTER turns your writing rhythm into cryptographic proof that a human authored your work.

Privacy by Design

Keystroke timing analysis runs entirely on your device. A privacy-preserving hash of writing metadata is sent to the signing server to generate your certificate. No raw document text is ever transmitted.

Real-Time Verification

As you write in Google Docs, JITTER learns your unique rhythm. The Dragon Guardian HUD shows your Humanity tier in real time.

Seamless Calibration

JITTER calibrates while you naturally type. No boring tests required. Just be yourself.

You’re All Set

Start writing and prove the value of your human creativity to the world.
